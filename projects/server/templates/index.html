How to create an event?

Open on the home page and tap Create an event. Or scroll down to the bottom of the homepage and tap the rectangle with . Purpletree will direct you to a form where you can submit a proposal of your event. After its submission, a review team will contact you through your provided email.

How to report a bug?

Open and tap Feedback . This will direct you to a form where you can describe the bug. We are thankful for your feedback.

Can I create a non-speaker event, like a dance, a concert, or a study break?

Certainly, you can create any event happening on campus. Purpletree can show time and location of your event. Though it usually features a speaker profile on the cover of an event page, showing speaker profiles is not necessary. Some events like dances or concerts are better advertised by pictures of a group of people or pictures of a stage set. After you submit your event proposal, you and our review team will both determine its visual design.

Blank images

Purpletree sometimes show blank images because it is offline and couldn't find relevant images on your phone. This usually happens because you have cleared its memory, or cache. The best solution is to refresh Purpletree by going to and tapping Refresh . However, if blank images happen too often, this may be a bug of Purpletree. Report the problem by going to > Feedback.

Why do I need to provide my email?

Purpletree needs your email for many reasons. When you subscribe to a club, Purpletree needs your email to add you to the mailing list. When you create an event, Purpletree needs your email to put you in contact with the event review team. In other cases, rarely, Purpletree will send you version updates and other product information.

Exclamation alert

When Purpletree shows a rectangle with , it has troubles finding an event. Tap on , you will find details of this problem. Usually this happens because you don't have an internet connection. Connect your phone to the internet and reload Purpletree. Less commonly, there really isn't any event. You can create more events by tapping .

What will happen after I create an event?

After you submitted your event proposal, you will receive an email confirming your submission. Later our review team will reach back to you to tell you whether your event is approved or whether some details are needed. This process generally takes place within a day. However, if you haven't heard back from us after a day, check the spam inbox of your email. If you couldn't find our emails anywhere, your proposal likely didn't get submitted or yor submitted a wrong email address. In either case, the best way is to submit another proposal.

How to save events to my calendar?

Open and toggle Enable Calendar Scheduling. This will allow Purpletree to save any event you have liked to your calendar app. When organizers change the time or location of your liked events, your calendar will get updated.

How to receive notifications about events?

By default, Purpletree will try to notify you 5 minutes before your liked events start. However, it can only show you notifications if you give it permission. You can allow notifications by going to the system app of your phone. Alternatively, you can go to > Allows notifications.
